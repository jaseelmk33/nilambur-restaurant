About Us

Discover our journey, values, and the people behind Nilambur Restaurant

Our Story

A Legacy of Flavor

Nilambur Restaurant was born from a deep passion for preserving and sharing the authentic culinary traditions of Kerala. Founded in 2018, our restaurant began as a dream to create a space where the rich flavors and warm hospitality of God's Own Country could be experienced by all.

Our founder, Chef Rashid, grew up in a family where cooking was not just about nourishment, but about bringing people together. The recipes we serve today have been passed down through generations, each dish telling a story of our heritage from the beautiful land of Nilambur.

From our humble beginnings, we have grown into a beloved destination for those seeking authentic Kerala cuisine in an atmosphere that celebrates our culture and traditions.

Since 2025

Our Values
